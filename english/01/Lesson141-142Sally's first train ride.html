# Lesson141 语法讲解-被动语态

被动语态的构成：be + done（过去分词）
be 都有：am、is、are、was、been、were、being

  1. 窗户被关了。

The window ___ 了.
被关 = is closed
The window is closed.

  1. 一般现在时的被动：is\am\are + done

他被杀了。
He 被 kill 了。
被杀=is killed
He is killed.

  1. 一般过去时的被动：was\were + done

他昨晚被杀了。
He 被 kill 了 last night.
昨晚被杀 = was killed
He was killed last night.

  1. 手表坏了。
    坏了= is broken
    The watch is broken.
  2. 她的钱包昨天丟了。
    昨天丟了 = was lost
    Her wallet was lost yesterday.
  3. 这几辆车是上周修好的。
    上周修好的 = were repaired
    The cars were repaired last week.
  4. 她儿子被送到学校去了。
    被送去了 = was taken
    Her son was taken to school.
  5. 我昨天被邀请去参加了他的生日聚会。

昨天被邀请 = was invited
I was invited to his birthday party yesterday.

  1. 没有人是生而知之者。

生而 = is born
No man is born wise or learned.

  1. 这里每天迎接成百上千的游客。

每天迎接 = is visited
It is visited by hundreds of people every day here.

  1. 何时使用被动语态？
  • 不知道动作的发出者
    It is broken.
  • 强调动作的发出者
    He breaks it. & It is broken by him.
  • 没必要提出动作的发出者
    A new building was built.
  1. 惯用的被动语态
  • be born... （出生：bear-bore-born)
    He was born in 1984.
  • be hurt ...（受伤：hurt-hurt-hurt)
    Tam hurt.
  • be dressed in（打扮：dress-dressed-dressed)
    The lady is dressed in a large funny hat

    My daughter is four years old.

学生阅览室：a students' reading room

  1. I decided to take her by train. 我決定带她坐火车去。

ddecide to do ...决定做.….
She decided to rent out a room to get extra income.
她为获得额外收入决定租出一个房问。

  1. Sally was very excited, because she had never traveled before.

  1. She sat near the window and asked questions about everything she saw.

everything she saw：她所见到的一切
他所做的一切：everything he did
他所说的事情：something he said
他所留下的一切：everything he left

  1. The lady was dressed in a blue coat and a large funny hat.

be dressed in.. 穿着..
He was dressed in a pair of black jeans.

  1. After the train had left the station, the lady opened her handbag and took out her powder compact.

蓝色字体为时间状语从句。

  1. She then began to make up her face.

make up one's face: 化妆

  1. To make myself beautiful.

为了把自己打扮的漂亮。

to... 表示目的
I am coming to see you.
I learn English to go abroad.
He did that to make her happy.
